William Pinkney Berry Family 1817



William Pinkney Berry was born 15 Dec 1817 in Franklin County, Tennessee.

His parents are unknown

He died in 1870 in Angelina County, Texas and is buried in the Berry Cemetery, Moffitt, Angelina County, Texas.

He married Nancy Elmirah Davis on 31 Dec 1871 (place is unknown).

Children of William Pinkney Berry and Nancy Elmirah Davis are:
  • A daughter, Nancy Susanna Berry was born 11 Apr 1874.

    • He married Susannah Davis on ABT 1878 (place is unknown).

    Children of William Pinkney Berry and Susannah Davis are:
  • A son, John Franklin Berry was born 5 Oct 1878.

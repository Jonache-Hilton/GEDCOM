Francis Ann Jones Family 1881



Francis Ann Jones was born 23 Dec 1881 in Lufkin, Angelina County, Texas.

She is the daughter of William Perry Jones and Mahaly Martha Harris.

She died in 27 Sep 1967 in Agra, Oklahoma and is buried in the Mashak Cemetery, Oklahoma County, Oklahoma.

She married William Andrew Isabell (unknown).

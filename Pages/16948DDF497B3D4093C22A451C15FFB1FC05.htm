James Riley Jones Family 1859



James Riley Jones was born 10 Mar 1859 in Texas.

He is the son of James Middleton Jones and Mary Ann Sparks.

He died in 6 Jun 1939 in Diaboll Angelina County Texas and is buried in the Ryan Chapel cemetery, Angelina County, Texas.

He married Georgia Minnie Sanders on 1902 (place is unknown).

No Children between James Riley Jones and Georgia Minnie Sanders

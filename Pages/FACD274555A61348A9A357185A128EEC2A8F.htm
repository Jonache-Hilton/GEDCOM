Ephriam P Anderson Family 1836



Ephriam P Anderson was born 1836 in Mississippi.

His parents are unknown

He died in 1870 in Angelina County, Texas and buried is unknown.

He married Nancy Elmirah Davis on 1 Sep 1856 in Texas

Children of Ephriam P Anderson and Nancy Elmirah Davis are:

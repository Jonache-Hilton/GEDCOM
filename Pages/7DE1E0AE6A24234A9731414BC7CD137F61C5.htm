Viola Jones Family 1878



Viola Jones was born 1 Nov 1878 in Angelina County, Texas.

She is the daughter of Oliver L Jones and Mary Jane Cochran.

She died in 15 Feb 1967 in Groves, Jefferson County, Texas and is buried in the Berry cemetery, Moffitt, Angelina County, Texas.

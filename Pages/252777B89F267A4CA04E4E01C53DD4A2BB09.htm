Charles Marvin McClanahan Family 1878



Charles Marvin McClanahan was born 13 Feb 1878 in Decatur, Wise County, Texas.

His parents are unknown

He died in 29 Dec 1936 in Oklahoma and is buried in the Old Graham cemetery, Carter, Beckham County, Oklahoma.

He married Sarah Margaret Jones on ABT 1898 (place is unknown).

No Children between Charles Marvin McClanahan and Sarah Margaret Jones

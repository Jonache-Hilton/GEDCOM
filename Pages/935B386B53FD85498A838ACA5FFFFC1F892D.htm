Julia B Jones Family 1896



Julia B Jones was born 12 Mar 1896 in Angelina County, Texas.

She is the daughter of Oliver L Jones and Mary Jane Cochran.

She died in 3 Feb 1975 in Nacogdoches County, Texas and is buried in the Sunset Memorial Park, Nacogdoches County, Texas.

She married Martin Junius Taylor Jr on 7 Feb 1916 in Angelina County, Texas

No Children between Martin Junius Taylor Jr and Julia B Jones

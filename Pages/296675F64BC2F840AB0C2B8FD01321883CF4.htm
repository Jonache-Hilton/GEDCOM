Della Lea Jones Family 1891



Della Lea Jones was born 20 Jun 1891 in Hardin County, Texas.

She is the daughter of Hayden Lane Jones and Mary Catherine Missouri Strahan.

She died in 31 Jul 1920 in Port Arthur, Jefferson, Texas and buried is unknown.

She married John Malcolm Rudd on 20 Nov 1912 in Jefferson County, Texas

No Children between John Malcolm Rudd and Della Lea Jones

Mary Catherine Missouri Strahan Family 1857



Mary Catherine Missouri Strahan was born 6 Dec 1857 in Nacogdoches County, Texas.

Her parents are unknown

She died in 17 Jan 1934 in Port Arthur, Jefferson County, Texas and is buried in the Greenlawn Memorial Park, Groves, Jefferson County, Texas.

She married Hayden Lane Jones on 14 Apr 1890 in Angelina County, Texas

Children of Hayden Lane Jones and Mary Catherine Missouri Strahan are:
  • A daughter, Della Lea Jones was born 20 Jun 1891.
  • A son, John Allen Jones was born 27 Sep 1893.
  • A daughter, Clementine Missouri Jones was born 16 Feb 1896.
  • A son, Hayden Edward Jones Sr was born 14 Nov 1900.

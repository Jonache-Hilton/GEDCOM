Martha Charlotte Wilkerson Family 1883



Martha Charlotte Wilkerson was born 2 Jan 1883 in Texas.

Her parents are unknown

She died in 3 Nov 1973 in Lufkin, Angelina County, Texas and is buried in the Berry Cemetery, Angelina County, Texas.

She married Andrew Jackson Berry on 22 Dec 1901 in Angelina County, Texas

Charles Brown Winstead Family 1775



Charles Brown Winstead was born 1775 in North Carolina.

He is the son of Samuel Winstead and Elizabeth Jones.

He died in 1835 in Virginia and buried is unknown.

He married Katherine Hall (date and place are unknown).

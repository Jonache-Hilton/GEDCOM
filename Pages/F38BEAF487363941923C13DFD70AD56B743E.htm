Radie Inez Tunnell Family 1912



Radie Inez Tunnell was born 15 Oct 1912 in Texas.

She is the daughter of Blueford Allen Tunnell and Ira Jones.

She died in 27 Jan 1984 in Woodville, Tyler County, Texas and buried is unknown.

She married William Benton Jordan on 25 Feb 1937 in Tyler County, Texas

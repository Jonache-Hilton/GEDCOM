Johnnie Louise Jones Family 1912



Johnnie Louise Jones was born 7 Oct 1912 in Pollock, Angelina County, Texas.

She is the daughter of Albert Alvin Jones and Lillian Margarette Selman.

She died in 10 Apr 1994 in Dayton, Liberty County, Texas and is buried in the Magnolia Park cemetery, Dayton, Liberty County, Texas.

She married William Anthony Weaver on 14 Nov 1957 (place is unknown).

James Middleton Jones Family 1819



James Middleton Jones was born 1819 in Tennessee.

He is the son of Joshua D Jones and Sarah P Stiles.

He died in 1900 in Texas and buried is unknown.

He married Mary Ann Sparks on 15 Feb 1849 in Angelina County, Texas

Children of James Middleton Jones and Mary Ann Sparks are:
  • A son, Joshua T Jones was born Sep 1854.
  • A daughter, Sarah Elizabeth Jones was born 20 May 1854.
  • A son, James Riley Jones was born 10 Mar 1859.
  • A son, George Jones was born 1859.

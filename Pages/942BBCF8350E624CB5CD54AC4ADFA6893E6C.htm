Susannah Davis Family 1845



Susannah Davis was born 1845 in Texas.

She is the daughter of Ashburn Davis and Hester Hetta.

She died in 1882 in Angelina County, Texas and buried is unknown.

She married William Pinkney Berry on ABT 1878 (place is unknown).

Children of William Pinkney Berry and Susannah Davis are:
  • A son, John Franklin Berry was born 5 Oct 1878.
  • A son, Andrew Jackson Berry was born 11 Feb 1882.

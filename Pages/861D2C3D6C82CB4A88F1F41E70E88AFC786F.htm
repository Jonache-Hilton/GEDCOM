Robert Melvin Jones Family 1878



Robert Melvin Jones was born 15 Jan 1878 in Lufkin, Angelina County, Texas.

He is the son of William Perry Jones and Mahaly Martha Harris.

He died in 21 May 1967 in Stockton San Joaquin County, California and is buried in the Highland View Memorial Park, San Joaquin, Ca.

He married Fannie Mae Montague (date and place are unknown).

No Children between Robert Melvin Jones and Fannie Mae Montague

Carrie Booker Family 1874



Carrie Booker was born Feb 1874 in Arkansas.

Her parents are unknown

She died in Sep 1936 in Oklahoma and is buried in the Clearsprings Mashak cemetery, Okc, Ok.

She married John Henry Jones on 27 Nov 1899 in Oklahoma

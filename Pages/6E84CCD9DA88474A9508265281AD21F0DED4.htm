George A Davis Family 1830



George A Davis was born 1830 in Tennessee.

He is the son of Ashburn Davis and Hester Hetta.

He died in ABT 1876 in Texas and buried is unknown.

He married Martha Anderson on 8 Aug 1849 in Angelina County, Texas

No Children between George A Davis and Martha Anderson

Martha Malinda Jones Family 1821



Martha Malinda Jones was born 12 Dec 1821 in Franklin, Tennessee.

She is the daughter of Joshua D Jones and Sarah P Stiles.

She died in 8 Jun 1896 in Dark Canyon, Eddy County, New Mexico and is buried in the X Bar Ranch, Mt Eddy County, New Mexico.

She married James Henry Kuykendall on 5 May 1844 in Nacogdoches, Texas

No Children between James Henry Kuykendall and Martha Malinda Jones

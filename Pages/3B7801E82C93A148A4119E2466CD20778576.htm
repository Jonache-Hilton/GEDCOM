Sarah Ann Davis Family 1832



Sarah Ann Davis was born 1832 in Tennessee.

She is the daughter of Ashburn Davis and Hester Hetta.

She died in 1870 in Angelina County, Texas and buried is unknown.

She married John Franklin Jones on 28 May 1846 in Nacogdoches, Angelina County, Texas

Children of John Franklin Jones and Sarah Ann Davis are:
  • A son, Hayden Lane Jones was born 2 Jan 1864.
  • A daughter, Margaret Ann Jones was born 1851.
  • A son, Oliver L Jones was born 14 Aug 1856.
  • A daughter, Mary Catherine Jones was born 10 Jan 1861.

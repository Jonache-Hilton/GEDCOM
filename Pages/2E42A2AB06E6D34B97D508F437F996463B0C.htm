Hazel Lorene Tunnell Family 1918



Hazel Lorene Tunnell was born 13 Apr 1918 in Deweyville, Newton County, Texas.

She is the daughter of Blueford Allen Tunnell and Ira Jones.

She died in 8 Mar 1991 in Woodville, Tyler County, Texas and is buried in the Beech Creek Cemetery, Spurger, Texas.

She married Jim Bob Perkins on 14 Jul 1935 in Woodville, Tyler County, Texas

No Children between Jim Bob Perkins and Hazel Lorene Tunnell

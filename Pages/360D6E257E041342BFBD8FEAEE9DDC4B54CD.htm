Anna Ruth Jones Family 1918



Anna Ruth Jones was born 2 May 1918 in Liberty County, Texas.

She is the daughter of Albert Alvin Jones and Lillian Margarette Selman.

She died in 1 Mar 1984 in Texas and is buried in the Sweeney cemetery, Sweeney, Brazoria County, Texas.

She married J B Whitmire (unknown).

No Children between J B Whitmire and Anna Ruth Jones

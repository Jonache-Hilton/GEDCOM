Ellender Rebecca Brookshire Family 1840

Her parents are unknown

She died in 15 Aug 1928 in Lufkin, Angelina County, Texas and is buried in the Berry cemetery, Moffitt, Angelina County, Texas.

She married James P Jones on 1860 in Angelina County, Texas

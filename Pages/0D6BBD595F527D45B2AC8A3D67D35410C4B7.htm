Joshua D Jones Family 1785



Joshua D Jones was born 1785 in Tennessee.

His parents are unknown

He died in 12 Apr 1847 in Angelina County, Texas and buried is unknown.

He married Sarah P Stiles on BEF 1815 in Possibly Tn

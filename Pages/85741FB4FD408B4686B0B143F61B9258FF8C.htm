Leonia Pearl O'Quinn Family 1914



Leonia Pearl O'Quinn was born 16 May 1914 in Rocky Hill, Texas.

Her parents are unknown

She died in 18 Jun 2003 in Dayton, Liberty County, Texas and is buried in the Greenville Cemetery, Kenefick, Liberty County, Texas.

She married Albert Alvin Jones (unknown).

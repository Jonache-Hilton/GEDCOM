Hade Leland Jones Family 1910



Hade Leland Jones was born 17 Oct 1910 in Manning, Angelina County, Texas.

He is the son of Albert Alvin Jones and Lillian Margarette Selman.

He died in 26 Nov 1971 in Liberty, Liberty County, Texas and is buried in the Magnolia Park, Dayton, Liberty County, Texas.

He married Lula Eugene Majors (date and place are unknown).

No Children between Hade Leland Jones and Lula Eugene Majors

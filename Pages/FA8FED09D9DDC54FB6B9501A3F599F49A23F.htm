Lillian Margarette Selman Family 1886



Lillian Margarette Selman was born 26 Feb 1886 in Texas.

Her parents are unknown

She died in 18 Dec 1968 in Liberty, Liberty County, Texas and is buried in the Magnolia Park Cemetery, Dayton, Liberty County, Texas.

She married Albert Alvin Jones on 30 Jun 1907 in Angelina County, Texas

Children of Albert Alvin Jones and Lillian Margarette Selman are:
  • A son, Joseph Weldon Jones was born 16 Aug 1908.
  • A son, Hade Leland Jones was born 17 Oct 1910.
  • A daughter, Johnnie Louise Jones was born 7 Oct 1912.
  • A daughter, Anna Ruth Jones was born 2 May 1918.
  • A son, Louie Hudson Jones was born 22 Jul 1921.

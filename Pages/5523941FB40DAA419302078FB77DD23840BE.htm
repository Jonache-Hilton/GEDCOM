Nancy Susanna Berry Family 1874



Nancy Susanna Berry was born 11 Apr 1874 in Texas.

She is the daughter of William Pinkney Berry and Nancy Elmirah Davis.

She died in 13 Jan 1934 in Rusk, Texas and is buried in the Berry Cemetery, Angelina County, Texas.

She married Richard E Parish on 24 Dec 1891 in Texas

No Children between Richard E Parish and Nancy Susanna Berry

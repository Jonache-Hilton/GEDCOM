Hayden Lane Jones Family 1864



Hayden Lane Jones was born 2 Jan 1864 in Angelina County, Texas.

He is the son of John Franklin Jones and Sarah Ann Davis.

He died in 14 Sep 1933 in Hardin County, Texas and is buried in the Greenlawn Memorial Park, Groves, Jefferson County, Texas.

He married Clementine Cleshia Berry on 8 Aug 1882 in Angelina County, Texas

Children of Hayden Lane Jones and Clementine Cleshia Berry are:
  • A son, Albert Alvin Jones was born 9 Aug 1884.
  • A daughter, Ira Jones was born 21 Jun 1888.

    • He married Mary Catherine Missouri Strahan on 14 Apr 1890 in Angelina County, Texas

    Children of Hayden Lane Jones and Mary Catherine Missouri Strahan are:
  • A daughter, Della Lea Jones was born 20 Jun 1891.
  • A son, John Allen Jones was born 27 Sep 1893.
  • A daughter, Clementine Missouri Jones was born 16 Feb 1896.
  • A son, Hayden Edward Jones Sr was born 14 Nov 1900.

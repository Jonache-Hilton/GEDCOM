Julia Ann Davis Family 1879



Julia Ann Davis was born 19 Mar 1879 in Rocky Hill, Angelina County, Texas.

She is the daughter of William D Y Davis and Sarah Jane Cole.

She died in 30 Aug 1963 in Forest, Cherokee County, Texas and is buried in the Thompson cemetery, Cherokee County, Texas.

She married Enoch Henry Latham (unknown).

No Children between Enoch Henry Latham and Julia Ann Davis

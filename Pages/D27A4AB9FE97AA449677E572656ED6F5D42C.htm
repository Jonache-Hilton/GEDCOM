Jemima Ann Victory Davis Family 1865



Jemima Ann Victory Davis was born 14 Jul 1865 in Bell County, Texas.

She is the daughter of William D Y Davis and Sarah Jane Cole.

She died in 15 Feb 1951 in Angelina County, Texas and is buried in the Freeman cemetery, Angelina County, Texas.

She married John A Freeman on 16 Feb 1890 (place is unknown).

No Children between John A Freeman and Jemima Ann Victory Davis

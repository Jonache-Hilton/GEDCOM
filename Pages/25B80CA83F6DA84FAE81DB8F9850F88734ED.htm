James P Jones Family 1842



James P Jones was born 12 Jan 1842 in Texas.

He is the son of John Franklin Jones and Sara A Brookshire.

He died in 18 Feb 1902 in Texas and is buried in the Berry cemetery, Moffitt, Angelina County, Texas.

He married Ellender Rebecca Brookshire on 1860 in Angelina County, Texas

No Children between James P Jones and Ellender Rebecca Brookshire

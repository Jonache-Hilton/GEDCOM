William D Y Davis Family 1836



William D Y Davis was born Jan 1836 in Tennessee.

He is the son of Ashburn Davis and Hester Hetta.

He died in 4 Dec 1900 in Forrest, Cherokee County, Texas and is buried in the Thompson cemetery, Cherokee County, Texas.

He married Sarah Jane Cole on 25 Jan 1860 in Angelina County, Texas

Children of William D Y Davis and Sarah Jane Cole are:
  • A son, Jesse William Davis was born 4 May 1861.
  • A daughter, Jemima Ann Victory Davis was born 14 Jul 1865.
  • A daughter, Elmina Davis was born 17 Nov 1871.
  • A daughter, Louisa Paralee Davis was born 14 Aug 1875.
  • A daughter, Julia Ann Davis was born 19 Mar 1879.

Louie Hudson Jones Family 1921



Louie Hudson Jones was born 22 Jul 1921 in Angelina County, Texas.

He is the son of Albert Alvin Jones and Lillian Margarette Selman.

He died in 13 Nov 1991 in Emory, Rains County, Texas and is buried in the Restlawn, Dallas, Dallas, Texas.

He married Syble Jack Stewart (date and place are unknown).

No Children between Louie Hudson Jones and Syble Jack Stewart

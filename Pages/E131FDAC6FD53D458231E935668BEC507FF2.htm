Henderson H Brookshire Family 1869



Henderson H Brookshire was born 17 Sep 1869 in Texas.

He is the son of Jesse Wright Brookshire and Margaret Ann Jones.

He died in 27 Mar 1912 in Angelina County, Texas and is buried in the Berry cemetery, Moffitt, Angelina County, Texas.

He married Minnie Ola Holland (date and place are unknown).

No Children between Henderson H Brookshire and Minnie Ola Holland

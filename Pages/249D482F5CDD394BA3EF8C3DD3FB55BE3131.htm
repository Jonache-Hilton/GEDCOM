Elmina Davis Family 1871



Elmina Davis was born 17 Nov 1871 in North Carolina.

She is the daughter of William D Y Davis and Sarah Jane Cole.

She died in 13 Dec 1944 in Wells, Cherokee County, Texas and is buried in the Thompson cemetery, Cherokee County, Texas.

She married Armond Cartwright on 14 Feb 1897 in Forest, Cherokee County, Texas

No Children between Armond Cartwright and Elmina Davis

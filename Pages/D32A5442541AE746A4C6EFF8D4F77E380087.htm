Clementine Missouri Jones Family 1896



Clementine Missouri Jones was born 16 Feb 1896 in Hardin County, Texas.

She is the daughter of Hayden Lane Jones and Mary Catherine Missouri Strahan.

She died in 19 Aug 1987 in Port Arthur, Jefferson, Texas and buried is unknown.

She married Eugene Berton Coker on 12 Sep 1923 in Jefferson County, Texas

No Children between Eugene Berton Coker and Clementine Missouri Jones

She married Henry Harrison York Sr (unknown).

Children of Henry Harrison York Sr and Clementine Missouri Jones are:
  • A daughter, Ruby Mae York was born 29 Oct 1914.
  • A son, Henry Harrison York Jr was born 10 Jul 1917.

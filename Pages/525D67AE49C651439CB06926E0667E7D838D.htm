Mary Catherine Jones Family 1861



Mary Catherine Jones was born 10 Jan 1861 in Angelina County, Texas.

She is the daughter of John Franklin Jones and Sarah Ann Davis.

She died in 1 Jan 1902 in Angelina County, Texas and is buried in the Traweek Cemetery, Angelina County, Texas.

She married Hosea Penn Traweek on 1880 in Angelina County, Texas

No Children between Hosea Penn Traweek and Mary Catherine Jones

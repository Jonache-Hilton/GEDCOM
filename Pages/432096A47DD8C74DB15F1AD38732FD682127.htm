William L Anderson Family 1867



William L Anderson was born 9 Dec 1867 in Texas.

He is the son of Ephriam P Anderson and Nancy Elmirah Davis.

He died in 17 Jan 1936 in Angelina County, Texas and is buried in the Rocky Hill cemetery, Angelina County, Texas.

He married Ellen Nevada Jones on 8 Jul 1883 in Angelina County, Texas

No Children between William L Anderson and Ellen Nevada Jones

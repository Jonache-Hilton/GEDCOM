Samuel Bumpas Family 1676



Samuel Bumpas was born 30 Apr 1676 in Plymouth County, Massachusetts.

He is the son of John Bumpas and Sarah Hunter.

He died in 18 Apr 1770 in Plymouth County, Massachusetts and buried is unknown.

He married Mary Gabriel (date and place are unknown).

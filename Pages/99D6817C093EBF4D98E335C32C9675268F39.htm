John Allen Jones Family 1893



John Allen Jones was born 27 Sep 1893 in Angelina County, Texas.

He is the son of Hayden Lane Jones and Mary Catherine Missouri Strahan.

He died in Mar 1947 in Port Arthur, Texas and is buried in the Forest Lawn Cemetery, Beaumont, Texas.

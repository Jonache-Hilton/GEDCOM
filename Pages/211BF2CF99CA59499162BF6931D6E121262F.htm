Louisa Paralee Davis Family 1875



Louisa Paralee Davis was born 14 Aug 1875 in Texas.

She is the daughter of William D Y Davis and Sarah Jane Cole.

She died in 1964 in Crockett, Houston County, Texas and is buried in the Thompson cemetery, Cherokee County, Texas.

She married William Wayne Bates on ABT 1897 in Texas

No Children between William Wayne Bates and Louisa Paralee Davis

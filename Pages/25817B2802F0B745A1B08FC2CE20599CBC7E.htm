Jesse William Davis Family 1861



Jesse William Davis was born 4 May 1861 in Angelina County, Texas.

He is the son of William D Y Davis and Sarah Jane Cole.

He died in 16 Oct 1932 in Angelina County, Texas and is buried in the Knight cemetery, Lufkin, Angelina County, Texas.

He married Felonia Katherine Goodwin on 19 Nov 1891 in Angelina County, Texas

No Children between Jesse William Davis and Felonia Katherine Goodwin

John Henry Jones Family 1872



John Henry Jones was born 16 Aug 1872 in Lufkin, Angelina County, Texas.

He is the son of William Perry Jones and Mahaly Martha Harris.

He died in 2 Jul 1956 in Norman, Cleveland County, Oklahoma and is buried in the Resthaven Gardens, Oklahome City, Cleveland County, Oklahoma.

He married Carrie Booker on 27 Nov 1899 in Oklahoma

No Children between John Henry Jones and Carrie Booker

He married Martha Cornelia Gross (date and place are unknown).

No Children between John Henry Jones and Martha Cornelia Gross

He married Genettie Settles on BEF 1894 (place is unknown).

No Children between John Henry Jones and Genettie Settles

Hayden Edward Jones Sr Family 1900



Hayden Edward Jones Sr was born 14 Nov 1900 in Angelina County, Texas.

He is the son of Hayden Lane Jones and Mary Catherine Missouri Strahan.

He died in 31 Jul 1970 in Tyler County, Texas and is buried in the Magnolia cemetery, Woodville, Tyler County, Texas.

He married Margie Mae Graham (date and place are unknown).

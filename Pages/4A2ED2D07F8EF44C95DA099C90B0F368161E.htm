Callie Davis Family 1859



Callie Davis was born Sep 1859 in Henderson County, Texas.

Her parents are unknown

She died in 24 Feb 1937 in Evant, Coryell County, Texas and is buried in the Murphree cemetery, Evant, Hamilton County, Texas.

She married Joshua George Massingill on 17 Jun 1879 in Smith County, Texas

No Children between Joshua George Massingill and Callie Davis

Clara Nell Herring Family 1915



Clara Nell Herring was born 28 Mar 1915 in Harris County, Texas.

Her parents are unknown

She died in 24 Dec 1992 in Dayton, Liberty County, Texas and is buried in the Magnolia cemetery, Dayton, Liberty County, Texas.

She married Joseph Weldon Jones on 10 Aug 1878 in Liberty County, Tx

No Children between Joseph Weldon Jones and Clara Nell Herring

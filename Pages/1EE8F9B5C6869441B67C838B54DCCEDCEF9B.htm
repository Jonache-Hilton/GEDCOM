Margaret Ann Jones Family 1851



Margaret Ann Jones was born 1851 in Angelina County, Texas.

She is the daughter of John Franklin Jones and Sarah Ann Davis.

She died in 1904 in Angelina County, Texas and buried is unknown.

She married Jesse Wright Brookshire on 28 May 1868 in Angelina County, Texas

Children of Jesse Wright Brookshire and Margaret Ann Jones are:
  • A son, Henderson H Brookshire was born 17 Sep 1869.
  • A son, Jesse C Brookshire was born 1867.
  • A daughter, Edna Jane Brookshire was born 1881.
  • A son, Worley Brookshire was born Aug 1883.
  • A daughter, Lula Brookshire was born 21 May 1892.

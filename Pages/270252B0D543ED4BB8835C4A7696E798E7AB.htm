William Perry Jones Family 1844



William Perry Jones was born 14 Apr 1844 in Bluefield Fort, Nacogdoches, Texas.

He is the son of John Franklin Jones and Sara A Brookshire.

He died in 22 Jul 1929 in Chickasha, Grady County, Oklahoma and is buried in the Rosehill Cemetery, Chickasha, Grady County, Oklahoma.

He married Mahaly Martha Harris on 27 Feb 1870 in Waco, Mclennan County. Texas

Children of William Perry Jones and Mahaly Martha Harris are:
  • A daughter, Ellen Nevada Jones was born 12 Jan 1867.
  • A son, George Hayden Jones was born 27 Dec 1870.
  • A son, John Henry Jones was born 16 Aug 1872.
  • A daughter, Penelopy Jones was born 12 Jan 1874.
  • A son, Robert Melvin Jones was born 15 Jan 1878.
  • A son, William Oscar Jones was born 5 Sep 1879.
  • A son, James Edgar Jones was born 23 Jul 1887.
  • A daughter, Francis Ann Jones was born 23 Dec 1881.
  • A daughter, Sarah Margaret Jones was born 24 Sep 1884.

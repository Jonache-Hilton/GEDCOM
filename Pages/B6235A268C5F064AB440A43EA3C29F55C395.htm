Ellen Nevada Jones Family 1867



Ellen Nevada Jones was born 12 Jan 1867 in Texas.

She is the daughter of William Perry Jones and Mahaly Martha Harris.

She died in 26 Jan 1947 in Lufkin, Angelina County, Texas and is buried in the Rocky Hill cemetery, Angelina County, Texas.

She married William L Anderson on 8 Jul 1883 in Angelina County, Texas

No Children between William L Anderson and Ellen Nevada Jones

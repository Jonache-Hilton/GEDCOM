Mary Sue Jones Family 1940



Mary Sue Jones was born 20 Mar 1940 in Angelina County, Texas.

She is the daughter of Albert Alvin Jones and Leonia Pearl O'Quinn.

She died in 26 Mar 2013 in Crockett, Texas and is buried in the Cremated.

She married Joseph O'Farrell Knight on 7 Apr 1962 (place is unknown).

No Children between Joseph O'Farrell Knight and Mary Sue Jones

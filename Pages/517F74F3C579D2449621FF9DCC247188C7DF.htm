Nancy Elmirah Davis Family 1838



Nancy Elmirah Davis was born 1838 in Mississippi.

She is the daughter of Ashburn Davis and Hester Hetta.

She died in 1878 in Angelina County, Texas and buried is unknown.

She married William Pinkney Berry on 31 Dec 1871 (place is unknown).

Children of William Pinkney Berry and Nancy Elmirah Davis are:
  • A daughter, Nancy Susanna Berry was born 11 Apr 1874.

    • She married Ephriam P Anderson on 1 Sep 1856 in Texas

    Children of Ephriam P Anderson and Nancy Elmirah Davis are:
  • A son, William L Anderson was born 9 Dec 1867.
  • A daughter, Eliza Jane Anderson was born 11 Nov 1859.

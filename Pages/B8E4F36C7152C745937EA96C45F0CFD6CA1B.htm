Jesse Wright Brookshire Family 1833



Jesse Wright Brookshire was born 5 Apr 1833 in Tennessee.

His parents are unknown

He died in 15 Dec 1904 in Angelina County, Texas and is buried in the Berry Cemetery, Moffitt, Angelina County, Texas.

He married Margaret Ann Jones on 28 May 1868 in Angelina County, Texas

Children of Jesse Wright Brookshire and Margaret Ann Jones are:
  • A son, Henderson H Brookshire was born 17 Sep 1869.
  • A son, Jesse C Brookshire was born 1867.
  • A daughter, Edna Jane Brookshire was born 1881.
  • A son, Worley Brookshire was born Aug 1883.
  • A daughter, Lula Brookshire was born 21 May 1892.

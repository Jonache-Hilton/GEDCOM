Sarah P Stiles Family 1800



Sarah P Stiles was born 1800 in Tennessee.

Her parents are unknown

She died in 1847 in Angelina County, Texas and buried is unknown.

She married Joshua D Jones on BEF 1815 in Possibly Tn

Children of Joshua D Jones and Sarah P Stiles are:
  • A son, John Franklin Jones was born 18 Oct 1818.
  • A son, James Middleton Jones was born 1819.
  • A daughter, Martha Malinda Jones was born 12 Dec 1821.
  • A daughter, Elizabeth Jones was born 1826.

Joshua George Massingill Family 1845



Joshua George Massingill was born 1845 in Lufkin, Angelina County, Texas.

He is the son of Isaac Massingill and Elizabeth Jones.

He died in Mar 1933 in Hamilton County, Texas and is buried in the Murphree cemetery, Evant, Hamilton County, Texas.

He married Callie Davis on 17 Jun 1879 in Smith County, Texas

No Children between Joshua George Massingill and Callie Davis

He married Harriett Moore (date and place are unknown).

No Children between Joshua George Massingill and Harriett Moore

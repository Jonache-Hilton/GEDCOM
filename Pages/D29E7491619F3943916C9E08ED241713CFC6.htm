Joseph Weldon Jones Family 1908



Joseph Weldon Jones was born 16 Aug 1908 in Pollock, Angelina County, Texas.

He is the son of Albert Alvin Jones and Lillian Margarette Selman.

He died in 6 Dec 1987 in Dayton, Liberty County, Texas and is buried in the Magnolia Park, Dayton, Liberty County, Texas.

He married Gladys Estelle Alexander on 3 Aug 1931 (place is unknown).

No Children between Joseph Weldon Jones and Gladys Estelle Alexander

He married Clara Nell Herring on 10 Aug 1878 in Liberty County, Tx

No Children between Joseph Weldon Jones and Clara Nell Herring

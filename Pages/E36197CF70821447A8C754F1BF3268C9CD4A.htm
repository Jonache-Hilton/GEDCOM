Eliza Jane Anderson Family 1859



Eliza Jane Anderson was born 11 Nov 1859 in Mississippi.

She is the daughter of Ephriam P Anderson and Nancy Elmirah Davis.

She died in 5 Mar 1890 in Angelina County, Texas and is buried in the Berry cemetery, Moffitt, Angelina County, Texas.

She married John Wright Mannering Brookshire on 21 Jul 1875 in Angelina County, Texas

No Children between John Wright Mannering Brookshire and Eliza Jane Anderson

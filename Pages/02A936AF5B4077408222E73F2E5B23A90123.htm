Charles Goodrich Gribble Family 1873



Charles Goodrich Gribble was born 1 May 1873 in Jefferson, Marion County, Texas.

His parents are unknown

He died in 2 Feb 1954 in Nacogdoches County, Texas and is buried in the Sunset Memorial Park, Nacogdoches County, Texas.

He married Madge Cochran Jones on 15 Feb 1911 in Angelina County, Texas

No Children between Charles Goodrich Gribble and Madge Cochran Jones

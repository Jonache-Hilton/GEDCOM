Elizabeth Jones Family 1826



Elizabeth Jones was born 1826 in Lincoln County, Tennessee.

She is the daughter of Joshua D Jones and Sarah P Stiles.

She died in 25 Nov 1878 in Hamilton County, Texas and is buried in the Gann cemetery Lower, Hamilton County, Texas.

She married Isaac Massingill on 4 May 1844 in Nacogdoches, Texas

Children of Isaac Massingill and Elizabeth Jones are:
  • A son, Joshua George Massingill was born 1845.
  • A son, Page M Massingill was born 1847.
  • A son, Isaac Massingill was born 1860.

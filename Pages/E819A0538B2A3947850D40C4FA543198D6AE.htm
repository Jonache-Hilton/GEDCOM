John Franklin Berry Family 1878



John Franklin Berry was born 5 Oct 1878 in Texas.

He is the son of William Pinkney Berry and Susannah Davis.

He died in 1901 in Texas and is buried in the Berry cemetery, Angelina County, Texas.

He married Eva Berry on 28 May 1900 (place is unknown).

No Children between John Franklin Berry and Eva Berry

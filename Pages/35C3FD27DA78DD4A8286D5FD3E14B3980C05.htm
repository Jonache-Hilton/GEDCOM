John Franklin Jones Family 1818



John Franklin Jones was born 18 Oct 1818 in Franklin County, Tennessee.

He is the son of Joshua D Jones and Sarah P Stiles.

He died in 1864 in Angelina County, Texas and is buried in the McKindree Cemetery, Angelina County, Texas.

He married Sarah Ann Davis on 28 May 1846 in Nacogdoches, Angelina County, Texas

Children of John Franklin Jones and Sarah Ann Davis are:
  • A son, Hayden Lane Jones was born 2 Jan 1864.
  • A daughter, Margaret Ann Jones was born 1851.
  • A son, Oliver L Jones was born 14 Aug 1856.
  • A daughter, Mary Catherine Jones was born 10 Jan 1861.

    • He married Sara A Brookshire on 1841 in Texas

    Children of John Franklin Jones and Sara A Brookshire are:
  • A son, James P Jones was born 12 Jan 1842.
  • A son, William Perry Jones was born 14 Apr 1844.
  • A son, John Henry Jones was born 8 Aug 1846.

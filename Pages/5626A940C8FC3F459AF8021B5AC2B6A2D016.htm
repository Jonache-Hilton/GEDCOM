Isaac Massingill Family 1825



Isaac Massingill was born 1 Feb 1825 in Greene County, Tennessee.

His parents are unknown

He died in 26 Dec 1901 in Hamilton County, Texas and is buried in the Gann cemetery Lower, Hamilton County, Texas.

He married Elizabeth Jones on 4 May 1844 in Nacogdoches, Texas

Children of Isaac Massingill and Elizabeth Jones are:
  • A son, Joshua George Massingill was born 1845.
  • A son, Page M Massingill was born 1847.
  • A son, Isaac Massingill was born 1860.

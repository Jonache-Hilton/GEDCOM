Blueford Allen Tunnell Family 1888



Blueford Allen Tunnell was born 23 Apr 1888 in Angelina County, Texas.

His parents are unknown

He died in 14 Jun 1921 in Jacksonville, Cherokee County, Texas and is buried in the Afton Grove-Vinson cem, Cherokee, Texas.

He married Ira Jones on 15 Feb 1905 in Angelina County, Texas

Children of Blueford Allen Tunnell and Ira Jones are:
  • A daughter, Hazel Lorene Tunnell was born 13 Apr 1918.
  • A son, Carey James Tunnell was born 18 Aug 1908.
  • A son, Carol Lee Tunnell was born 26 Jul 1910.
  • A daughter, Frankie Emily Tunnell was born 10 Mar 1913.
  • A daughter, Radie Inez Tunnell was born 15 Oct 1912.

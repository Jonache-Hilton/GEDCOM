Mary Ann Sparks Family 1835



Mary Ann Sparks was born 1835 in Mississippi.

Her parents are unknown

She died in AFT 1872 in Texas and buried is unknown.

She married James Middleton Jones on 15 Feb 1849 in Angelina County, Texas

Children of James Middleton Jones and Mary Ann Sparks are:
  • A son, Joshua T Jones was born Sep 1854.
  • A daughter, Sarah Elizabeth Jones was born 20 May 1854.
  • A son, James Riley Jones was born 10 Mar 1859.
  • A son, George Jones was born 1859.

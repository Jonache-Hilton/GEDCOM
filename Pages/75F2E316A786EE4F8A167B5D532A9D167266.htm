Alpheus Morgan Lusk Family 1862

His parents are unknown

He died in 5 Jun 1924 in Chickasha, Grady County, Oklahoma and is buried in the Rosehill Cemetery. Chickasha, Grady County, Oklahoma.

He married Penelopy Jones on 4 Feb 1890 (place is unknown).

Carey James Tunnell Family 1908



Carey James Tunnell was born 18 Aug 1908 in Texas.

He is the son of Blueford Allen Tunnell and Ira Jones.

He died in 12 Jan 1990 in Texas and is buried in the Beech Creek Baptist cemetery, Spurger, Tyler County, Texas.

He married Ruby Elizabeth Jones (date and place are unknown).

No Children between Carey James Tunnell and Ruby Elizabeth Jones

Richard E Parish Family 1868



Richard E Parish was born 27 Oct 1868 in Texas.

His parents are unknown

He died in 8 Jun 1903 in Lufkin, Angelina County, Texas and is buried in the Berry cemetery, Angelina County, Texas.

He married Nancy Susanna Berry on 24 Dec 1891 in Texas

Armond Cartwright Family 1849



Armond Cartwright was born Jan 1849 in North Carolina.

His parents are unknown

He died in 26 Dec 1921 in Forest, Cherokee County, Texas and is buried in the Thompson cemetery, Cherokee County, Texas.

He married Elmina Davis on 14 Feb 1897 in Forest, Cherokee County, Texas

No Children between Armond Cartwright and Elmina Davis

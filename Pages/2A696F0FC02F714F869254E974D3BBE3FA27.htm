Felonia Katherine Goodwin Family 1864



Felonia Katherine Goodwin was born 22 Mar 1864 in Haynesville, Claiborne Parish, Louisiana.

Her parents are unknown

She died in 12 Mar 1925 in Angelina County, Texas and is buried in the Knight cemetery, Lufkin, Angelina County, Texas.

She married Jesse William Davis on 19 Nov 1891 in Angelina County, Texas

No Children between Jesse William Davis and Felonia Katherine Goodwin

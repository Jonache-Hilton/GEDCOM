Ruby Mae York Family 1914



Ruby Mae York was born 29 Oct 1914 in Port Arthur, Jefferson County, Texas.

She is the daughter of Henry Harrison York Sr and Clementine Missouri Jones.

She died in 6 May 2011 in Beaumont, Jefferson County, Texas and is buried in the Greenlawn Memorial Park, Groves, Jefferson County, Texas.

She married Gunnar Utvik (unknown).

No Children between Gunnar Utvik and Ruby Mae York

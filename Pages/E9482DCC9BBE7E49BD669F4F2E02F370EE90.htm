Willie Don Jones Family 1947



Willie Don Jones was born 12 Aug 1947 in Liberty County, Texas.

He is the son of Albert Alvin Jones and Leonia Pearl O'Quinn.

He died in 25 Apr 2001 in Liberty County, Texas and is buried in the Greenville Cemetery, Kinifick, Liberty County, Texas.

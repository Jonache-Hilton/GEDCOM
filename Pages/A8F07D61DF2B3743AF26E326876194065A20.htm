Sarah Elizabeth Jones Family 1854



Sarah Elizabeth Jones was born 20 May 1854 in Texas.

She is the daughter of James Middleton Jones and Mary Ann Sparks.

She died in 1940 in Texas and is buried in the Ryan Chapel cemetery, Angelina County, Texas.

William Oscar Jones Family 1879



William Oscar Jones was born 5 Sep 1879 in Texas.

He is the son of William Perry Jones and Mahaly Martha Harris.

He died in 29 Nov 1943 in Sand Springs, Oklahoma and buried is unknown.

He married Bertha Mae Brant (date and place are unknown).

No Children between William Oscar Jones and Bertha Mae Brant

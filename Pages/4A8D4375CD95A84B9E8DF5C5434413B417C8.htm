Henry Harrison York Sr Family 1880



Henry Harrison York Sr was born 18 Apr 1880 in North Carolina.

His parents are unknown

He died in 25 Nov 1920 in Groves, Jefferson, Texas and is buried in the Greenlawn Memorial Park, Groves, Jefferson County, Texas.

He married Clementine Missouri Jones (date and place are unknown).

Children of Henry Harrison York Sr and Clementine Missouri Jones are:
  • A daughter, Ruby Mae York was born 29 Oct 1914.
  • A son, Henry Harrison York Jr was born 10 Jul 1917.

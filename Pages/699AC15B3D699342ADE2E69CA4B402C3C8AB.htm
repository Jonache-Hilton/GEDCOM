Ashburn Davis Family 1794



Ashburn Davis was born 1794 in Person, North Carolina.

He is the son of John Davey and Elizabeth Winstead.

He died in 1878 in Anna, Illinois and buried is unknown.

He married Hester Hetta on BEF 1830 (place is unknown).

Children of Ashburn Davis and Hester Hetta are:
  • A daughter, Nancy Elmirah Davis was born 1838.
  • A son, William D Y Davis was born Jan 1836.
  • A son, George A Davis was born 1830.
  • A daughter, Susannah Davis was born 1845.
  • A daughter, Francis B Davis was born 1850.
  • A daughter, Sarah Ann Davis was born 1832.

Cassie Orell Jones Family 1888



Cassie Orell Jones was born 10 Oct 1888 in Angelina County, Texas.

She is the daughter of Oliver L Jones and Mary Jane Cochran.

She died in 20 Aug 1968 in Beaumont, Jefferson County, Texas and is buried in the Zavalla cemetery, Angelina County, Texas.

She married Erna Rothchild Miles Sr (unknown).

No Children between Erna Rothchild Miles Sr and Cassie Orell Jones

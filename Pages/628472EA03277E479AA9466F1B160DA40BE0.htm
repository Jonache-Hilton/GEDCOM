Lula Brookshire Family 1892



Lula Brookshire was born 21 May 1892 in Texas.

She is the daughter of Jesse Wright Brookshire and Margaret Ann Jones.

She died in 19 Jan 1982 in Beaumont, Jefferson County, Texas and is buried in the Berry cemetery, Moffitt, Angelina County, Texas.

She married Quitman Quisenant (unknown).

No Children between Quitman Quisenant and Lula Brookshire

Carol Lee Tunnell Family 1910



Carol Lee Tunnell was born 26 Jul 1910 in Lufkin, Angelina County, Texas.

He is the son of Blueford Allen Tunnell and Ira Jones.

He died in 5 Feb 1976 in Houston, Harris County, Texas and is buried in the Beech Creek Baptist cemetery, Spurger, Tyler County, Texas.

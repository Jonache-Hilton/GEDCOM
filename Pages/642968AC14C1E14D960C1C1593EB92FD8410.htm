Mary Ann McCurdy Family 1858



Mary Ann McCurdy was born 21 Oct 1858 in DeKalb County, Alabama.

Her parents are unknown

She died in ABT 1944 in Hamilton County, Texas and is buried in the Gann cemetery Lower, Hamilton County, Texas.

She married Page M Massingill (unknown).

No Children between Page M Massingill and Mary Ann McCurdy

Oliver L Jones Family 1856



Oliver L Jones was born 14 Aug 1856 in Angelina County, Texas.

He is the son of John Franklin Jones and Sarah Ann Davis.

He died in 26 Feb 1899 in Angelina County, Texas and is buried in the Berry cemetery, Moffitt, Angelina County, Texas.

He married Mary Jane Cochran (date and place are unknown).

Children of Oliver L Jones and Mary Jane Cochran are:
  • A daughter, Viola Jones was born 1 Nov 1878.
  • A daughter, Cassie Orell Jones was born 10 Oct 1888.
  • A daughter, Madge Cochran Jones was born 11 Feb 1891.
  • A daughter, Julia B Jones was born 12 Mar 1896.

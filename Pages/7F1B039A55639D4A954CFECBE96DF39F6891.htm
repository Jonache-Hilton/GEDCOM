Worley Brookshire Family 1883



Worley Brookshire was born Aug 1883 in Angelina County, Texas.

He is the son of Jesse Wright Brookshire and Margaret Ann Jones.

He died in 1961 in Nacogdoches, Texas and buried is unknown.

He married Gertrude Tubbe (date and place are unknown).

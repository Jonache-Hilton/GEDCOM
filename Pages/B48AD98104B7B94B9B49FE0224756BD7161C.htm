Hayden Edward Jones Jr Family 1929



Hayden Edward Jones Jr was born 19 Jan 1929 in Port Arthur, Jefferson County, Texas.

He is the son of Hayden Edward Jones Sr and Margie Mae Graham.

He died in 19 Feb 2017 in Tyler County, Texas and is buried in the Greenlawn Memorial Park, Groves, Jefferson County, Texas.

Frankie Emily Tunnell Family 1913



Frankie Emily Tunnell was born 10 Mar 1913 in Texas.

She is the daughter of Blueford Allen Tunnell and Ira Jones.

She died in 1 Jul 1990 in Hillister, Tyler County, Texas and is buried in the Beech Creek Baptist cemetery, Spurger, Tyler County, Texas.

She married Jonas S English (unknown).

Ira Jones Family 1888



Ira Jones was born 21 Jun 1888 in Angelina County, Texas.

She is the daughter of Hayden Lane Jones and Clementine Cleshia Berry.

She died in 30 Aug 1952 in Spurger, Tyler County, Texas and is buried in the Beech Creek Baptist Cemetery, Spurger, Tyler, Texas.

She married Blueford Allen Tunnell on 15 Feb 1905 in Angelina County, Texas

Children of Blueford Allen Tunnell and Ira Jones are:
  • A daughter, Hazel Lorene Tunnell was born 13 Apr 1918.
  • A son, Carey James Tunnell was born 18 Aug 1908.
  • A son, Carol Lee Tunnell was born 26 Jul 1910.
  • A daughter, Frankie Emily Tunnell was born 10 Mar 1913.
  • A daughter, Radie Inez Tunnell was born 15 Oct 1912.

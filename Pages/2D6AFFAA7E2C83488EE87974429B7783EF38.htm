John Henry Jones Family 1846



John Henry Jones was born 8 Aug 1846 in Angelina County, Texas.

He is the son of John Franklin Jones and Sara A Brookshire.

He died in 7 Apr 1929 in Gypsy, Harrison, West Virginia and is buried in the Masonic cemetery, Harrison County, West Virginia.

He married Margaret C Reynolds on 26 Oct 1876 in Angelina County, Texas

No Children between John Henry Jones and Margaret C Reynolds

Page M Massingill Family 1847



Page M Massingill was born 1847 in Texas.

He is the son of Isaac Massingill and Elizabeth Jones.

He died in 1934 in Hamilton County, Texas and is buried in the Gann cemetery Lower, Hamilton County, Texas.

He married Mary Ann McCurdy (date and place are unknown).

No Children between Page M Massingill and Mary Ann McCurdy

He married Caldonia Sarah Underwood on 1872 in Angelina County, Texas

No Children between Page M Massingill and Caldonia Sarah Underwood

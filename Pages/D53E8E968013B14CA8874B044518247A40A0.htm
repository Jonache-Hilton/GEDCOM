Sarah Margaret Jones Family 1884



Sarah Margaret Jones was born 24 Sep 1884 in Texas.

She is the daughter of William Perry Jones and Mahaly Martha Harris.

She died in 1965 in Oklahoma and buried is unknown.

She married Charles Marvin McClanahan on ABT 1898 (place is unknown).

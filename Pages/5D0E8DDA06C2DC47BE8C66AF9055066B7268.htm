Madge Cochran Jones Family 1891



Madge Cochran Jones was born 11 Feb 1891 in Angelina County, Texas.

She is the daughter of Oliver L Jones and Mary Jane Cochran.

She died in 17 May 1957 in Nacogdoches County, Texas and is buried in the Sunset Memorial Park, Nacogdoches County, Texas.

She married Charles Goodrich Gribble on 15 Feb 1911 in Angelina County, Texas

No Children between Charles Goodrich Gribble and Madge Cochran Jones

James Henry Kuykendall Family 1818



James Henry Kuykendall was born 30 Nov 1818 in Franklin County, Tennessee.

His parents are unknown

He died in 15 Aug 1889 in Carlsbad, Eddy County, New Mexico and is buried in the X Bar Ranch, Mt Eddy County, New Mexico.

He married Martha Malinda Jones on 5 May 1844 in Nacogdoches, Texas

No Children between James Henry Kuykendall and Martha Malinda Jones

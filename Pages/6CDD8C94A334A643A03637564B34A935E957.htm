Mary Jane Cochran Family 1859



Mary Jane Cochran was born 1859 in Alabama.

Her parents are unknown

She died in 1907 in Angelina County, Texas and is buried in the Berry cemetery, Moffitt, Angelina County, Texas.

She married Oliver L Jones (unknown).

Children of Oliver L Jones and Mary Jane Cochran are:
  • A daughter, Viola Jones was born 1 Nov 1878.
  • A daughter, Cassie Orell Jones was born 10 Oct 1888.
  • A daughter, Madge Cochran Jones was born 11 Feb 1891.
  • A daughter, Julia B Jones was born 12 Mar 1896.

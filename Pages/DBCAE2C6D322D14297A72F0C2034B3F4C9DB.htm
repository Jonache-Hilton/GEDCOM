Penelopy Jones Family 1874



Penelopy Jones was born 12 Jan 1874 in Texas.

She is the daughter of William Perry Jones and Mahaly Martha Harris.

She died in 21 Feb 1937 in Chickasha, Grady County, Oklahoma and is buried in the Rosehill Cemetery. Chickasha, Grady County, Oklahoma.

She married Alpheus Morgan Lusk on 4 Feb 1890 (place is unknown).

No Children between Alpheus Morgan Lusk and Penelopy Jones
